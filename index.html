DJMAX RESPECT V

DJMAX RESPECT V is the latest installment from the creators of the critically acclaimed rhythm game franchise ‘DJMAX.’ Experience tracks from popular artists such as Marshmello, Porter Robinson, and Yukika, exclusive Full HD Music Videos, online multiplayer, new game modes, and more!

It delivers an unrivaled rhythm game experience, introducing new modes, new artists, more than 150 unique tracks, exclusive music videos, and for the first time in franchise history, competitive online-multiplayer!

Experience new tracks and music videos from world renowned artists like Marshmello, Porter Robinson, and Yukika, as well as the return of friendly names including BEXTER, ND LEE, Paul Bazooka, and Makou, among others.

Gameplay

Fabulous. Unlike other rythm games, DJMAX provides way to the common contribution of man to music. When you play the game, you are making your own music. Don’t believe this? Go to any gameplay video, and you’ll mostly find people tapping according to the music, and if they do fail in syncing to the beat, the music goes way off. You’ll feel the need to improve your rhythm, and get better fast that way. I was never the greatest fan of OSU!Mania, since it is just way too difficult, challenging and non-sync to the music. But that’s how it’d feel when you play on insane difficulties.

DJMAX provides you with options, from 4 to 8 keys of gameplay. You tap the keys to their custom-production made maps, which can be described as just, Elegant. You can keep your combo from breaking by playing maps on full-combo, and not exiting a map while you’re just playing. I’ve seen people keep their combo to like, 100k and stuff. There is also a lot of modes available, including Competitve (Self-Explanatory) and AIR mode, where you get to interact with the audience, and your messages display on the layout for a time.
